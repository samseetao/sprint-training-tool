Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

  • GPS Location: Used solely to calculate distance and provide audio prompts during training.
  • Session Records: Time, distance, and user notes saved locally in your browser.

All data is used exclusively to provide the training functionality of Sprint Training Tool. We do not share your data with third parties. Session data is stored locally on your device.
